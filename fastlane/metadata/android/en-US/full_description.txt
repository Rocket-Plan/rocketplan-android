RocketPlan is a mobile field documentation app for restoration contractors (water damage, fire damage, disaster restoration).                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  It helps restoration teams:                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
  - Document property losses with photos, notes, and damage assessments                                                                                                                                                                             
  - Manage restoration projects from initial assessment through completion                                                                                                                                                                          
  - Track drying equipment deployment and moisture readings                                                                                                                                                                                         
  - Capture thermal images via FLIR camera integration                                                                                                                                                                                              
  - Work offline with automatic sync when connectivity returns                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  The app streamlines field work so restoration companies can capture better documentation and get paid faster.
